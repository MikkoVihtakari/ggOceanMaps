Mikko Vihtakari (mikko.vihtakari@hi.no) is the sole author and maintainer of ggOceanMaps. He works at the Institute of Marine Research (Norway). The package grew from an earlier package called PlotSvalbard.

He is clearly an experienced R developer: he uses roxygen2, pkgdown, devtools, testthat, GitHub Actions CI, and has published the package on CRAN. Domain expertise in ocean sciences (bathymetry, spatial coordinate projections, Arctic/Antarctic mapping).

When helping, assume deep R knowledge, familiarity with sf/stars/ggplot2 spatial ecosystem, and that marine/oceanographic terminology is understood.
